What is sand art?

Live performance, drawn by hand.

Sand art is a live visual performance. Working over a backlit glass table, I draw with my hands, pouring, sweeping and shaping fine sand into images that flow seamlessly from one scene into the next.

Projected onto a large screen and set to music, every movement becomes part of a story your audience watches unfold in real time. Nothing is pre-recorded. Each show is created live, in the moment, and never exactly the same twice.

The artist

Meet Bilal Köse

I'm a UK-based professional sand artist with over 15 years of artistic experience, specialising in live sand art performances for events across the country and beyond. As Britain's first established resident sand animation artist, I'm one of very few performing this art form permanently from within the UK, rather than travelling in for one-off bookings.

Each performance is carefully tailored to reflect the message, story or values of the event, transforming a single moment into a memorable visual experience. Alongside sand art I also work with calligraphy and traditional techniques, which add depth and originality to every show.

I regularly perform at universities, theatres, museums, cathedrals, care homes and public venues, and offer educational workshops for children and adults. My goal is simple: to create art that isn't only seen, but truly felt.
